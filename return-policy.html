Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Defective Products Only

At AutoGear Ke, we stand behind the quality of our products. We only accept returns for items that are defective upon delivery or have manufacturing faults.

Return Eligibility

A product is eligible for return if:

  • The item has a manufacturing defect or arrived damaged
  • The item does not match the description or photos on our website
  • The item is non-functional or fails to perform as advertised
  • You report the issue within 7 days of delivery

To qualify, you must:

  • Provide photo evidence of the defect or damage
  • Retain all original packaging and accessories
  • Have your order number or proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:

  • Used or installed products
  • Items changed or modified by customer
  • Products with physical damage (drops, impacts)
  • Water damage or liquid exposure
  • Products damaged due to misuse
  • Items with missing tags or packaging
  • "As-is" or clearance items
  • Change of mind purchases

Return Process
